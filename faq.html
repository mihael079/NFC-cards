What You Can Do with Tap Tap
What happens if Guest chose "Answer Me"?
The Guest answers the Host’s questions and evaluates them. The system analyzes both parties.
What happens if Guest chose "Challenge Me"?
The Guest sets a challenge and critiques the Host’s performance. Mutual learning follows.
What happens if Guest chose "See My Persona Card"?
The Guest views the Host’s Persona Card and shares impressions. System feedback is generated.
